# Privacy Policy for Secure ID Scanner

Last updated: March 2026

Secure ID Scanner respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely on your device.

## No Data Collection

Secure ID Scanner does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.

The app does not use analytics, tracking, or third-party services.

## On-Device Processing

All features of the app operate **locally on your device**, including:

- Document scanning
- Blurring sensitive information
- Adding watermarks
- Saving and sharing documents

Your documents are **never uploaded to external servers**.

## Photos Access

If you choose to save a scanned document, the image will be stored in your **Photos library on your device**.

Secure ID Scanner does not access your photo library unless you explicitly grant permission.

## Camera Access

The app requires **camera access** to scan documents.

Images captured by the camera are processed directly on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be posted on this page.
