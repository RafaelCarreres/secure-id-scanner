# Support for Secure ID Scanner

Thank you for using **Secure ID Scanner**!

## Privacy First

Secure ID Scanner is designed with privacy in mind.  
All scanning, blurring, watermarking, and sharing happens **locally on your device**.

Your documents are **not uploaded, stored, or shared with external servers**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where are my documents stored?**  
A: Scanned documents are saved to your **Photos library** on your device.

**Q: Does the app require an internet connection?**  
A: No. The app works completely offline.

**Q: Do you collect any data?**  
A: No. Secure ID Scanner does **not collect, store, or transmit personal data**.

**Q: How do I blur sensitive information?**  
A: After scanning, simply **swipe your finger over the areas** you want to blur.

**Q: Can I remove watermarks?**  
A: Yes. **Double-tap a watermark** to remove it.
